# Directive: Plain Hindi / स्पष्ट हिंदी (General Accessibility)

## Basis [M]
**Type:** Adapted Standard
**Framework:** Inspired by Plain Language principles, adapted for Hindi
**Script:** Devanagari (देवनागरी)
**Target Level:** B1-B2 comprehension

---

## Purpose [M]
Defines rules for translating texts into Plain Hindi (स्पष्ट हिंदी) for general accessibility.

**Priority:** Clarity and readability for a broad audience. [M]
**Inspiration:** International Plain Language principles adapted for Hindi.

## When to Use [M]
- For general audience communication
- For administrative and government texts
- For public information materials
- When Easy Hindi is too simplified

---

## Key Requirements [M]

### The Hybrid Rule [M]
Technical terms **may be preserved** with inline explanations when necessary for accuracy.

#### Inline Explanation Format [M]
Use parentheses or dashes for brief explanations.

**Format:**
> दक्षता (काम को कम समय में करना) महत्वपूर्ण है।

> प्रोटोकॉल - यानी निर्धारित नियम - का पालन करें।

### Grammar & Sentence Structure [M]
- **Short sentences preferred** (15-20 words max) [M]
- **SOV order** (Subject - Object - Verb) - Hindi natural order [M]
- **Active voice preferred**, passive acceptable when natural [C]
- **Positive statements preferred** [C]

### Hindi-Specific Rules [M]

#### A. Register Balance [M]
Use a balanced mix of Hindi vocabulary:
- Prefer commonly understood words
- Sanskrit-heavy words acceptable if widely known
- Avoid overly colloquial language

#### B. Postpositions [M]
Keep postpositions in natural positions.

#### C. Verb Agreement [M]
Maintain proper gender and number agreement.

### Formatting [M]
- Use paragraphs to group related ideas [M]
- Use bullet points for lists [C]
- Use headings for long documents [C]

---

## Edge Cases & Special Rules [M]

### A. Word Choice [M]
Prefer commonly understood words over rare synonyms.

**Examples:**
| Less Common | Preferred |
|-------------|-----------|
| क्रय | खरीदना |
| विक्रय | बेचना |
| गृह | घर |
| जल | पानी |

### B. Abbreviations [M]
Spell out on first use, then abbreviation is acceptable.

**Example:**
> भारतीय रिज़र्व बैंक (RBI) ने नई नीति घोषित की। RBI की यह नीति...

### C. Technical Terms [M]
Explain on first use, then use freely.

**Example:**
> मुद्रास्फीति (जब चीजों की कीमतें बढ़ती हैं) एक समस्या है। मुद्रास्फीति को नियंत्रित करना जरूरी है।

### D. English Loanwords [M]
Commonly used English words are acceptable.
Provide Devanagari transliteration for clarity.

**Examples:**
> ऑनलाइन (Online)
> डिजिटल (Digital)

### E. Numbers and Dates [M]
- Arabic numerals (1, 2, 3) are acceptable
- Dates: 1 जनवरी 2024 or 1/1/2024

---

## Devanagari Script Rules [M]

### A. Standard Script [M]
Use standard Devanagari orthography.

### B. Punctuation [M]
- पूर्ण विराम (।) preferred for formal texts
- Period (.) acceptable in modern contexts
- Standard punctuation (?, !, :) acceptable
