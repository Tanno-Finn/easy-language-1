# Directive: Spanish Plain Language (Lenguaje Claro)

**ID:** `lenguaje-claro-business`
**Target Audience:** People with reading difficulties, Spanish as second language, or readers who want to grasp information quickly (scanning).
**Level:** A2/B1 (Fluent and comprehensible text).

---

## 1. Core Principles
Unlike Easy Read (Lectura Fácil), **flowing prose is allowed** here. The text appears more professional and adult, but remains maximally comprehensible.

### When to Use?
* For company-wide agreements for all employees.
* For emails, intranet news, or training for mixed teams.
* When Easy Read would be perceived as "too childish".

---

## 2. Language Rules

### A. Sentence Structure and Grammar
* **Sentence length:** Average 15 words. Maximum 20.
* **Subordinate clauses:** Allowed, but maximum one per sentence (no nested sentences).
    * *Good:* "Por favor avísenos si tiene preguntas."
    * *Bad:* "Por favor avísenos si tiene preguntas que se refieran al proyecto que empezamos ayer y que durará hasta fin de mes."
* **Passive voice:** Use sparingly. Active voice is always better.
    * *Acceptable:* "La solicitud será procesada."
    * *Better:* "Procesaremos su solicitud."

### B. Technical Terms and Legal Aspects (Business Context)
The strict Hybrid Rule from Easy Read ("Eso significa:") does not apply here.
* **Technical terms:** May be used.
* **Explanation:** Explain difficult terms casually in context or in a subordinate clause.
* **Example:** "Por favor respete las normas de Compliance, es decir, nuestras reglas para el comportamiento correcto en la empresa."

---

## 3. Edge Cases and Formatting

### A. Anglicisms
In business contexts, common Anglicisms are acceptable in Plain Language if established.
* *Acceptable:* Meeting, feedback, online, email.
* *Avoid:* "Vamos a brainstormear esto", "Dar el ok". -> *Better:* "Vamos a generar ideas", "Aprobar".

### B. Abbreviations
Common abbreviations (p. ej., etc., Dr., Sr.) are allowed.
Unknown or internal abbreviations (e.g., "conf. Art. 5 LPD") must be written out or explained on first occurrence.

### C. Long Words
No need for hyphens, but recommended for words over 20 letters.
* *Acceptable:* corresponsabilidad
* *Better:* co-responsabilidad

### D. Inclusive Language
Use neutral formulations or double form. No special symbols (@, x, e) as they hinder reading flow.
* *Good:* "Todo el personal", "El equipo", "Los empleados y las empleadas".

### E. Formatting
* Use subheadings to structure text.
* Important information can be marked in **bold**.
* Use bullet lists for 3 or more points.

---

## 4. Examples (Comparison)

### Example 1: Legal Notice
**Original:**
"Las infracciones contra las disposiciones de seguridad pueden acarrear consecuencias laborales."

**Plain Language (B1):**
"Si usted no cumple con las normas de seguridad, habrá consecuencias laborales. En el peor caso puede recibir una amonestación o ser despedido."

**(Comparison Easy Read A2 - for information only):**
"Siga las reglas.
Si no lo hace:
Tendrá problemas."

### Example 2: IT Instruction
**Original:**
"Tras la autenticación exitosa en el backend, el registro de datos debe ser validado."

**Plain Language (B1):**
"Inicie sesión en el sistema. Después debe verificar que los datos sean correctos."

---

## 5. Checklist before Delivery

1.  [ ] Is the tone respectful and adult (not childish)?
2.  [ ] Have nested sentences been eliminated?
3.  [ ] Have internal technical terms been briefly explained (without interrupting reading flow)?
4.  [ ] Is the text visually structured (paragraphs, subheadings)?

---

## 6. Documentation & QA Log

Add at the **end** of every translated file a brief QA appendix.
This is shorter than for Easy Read since rules are less strict.

**Format:**

```markdown
---

### QA-Log

**Technical terms used:**
- [Short list of main technical terms in the text]

**Language Level Check:**
- Nested sentences avoided: [Yes/No]
- Average sentence length: [approx. X words]
```

**Example:**
```markdown
---

### QA-Log

**Technical terms used:**
- Usabilidad, Experiencia de Usuario, Efectividad, Eficiencia, Satisfacción, Contexto de Uso

**Language Level Check:**
- Nested sentences avoided: Yes
- Average sentence length: approx. 14 words
```
