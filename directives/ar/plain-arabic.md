# Directive: اللغة العربية الواضحة (Arabic Plain Language) [M]

## Purpose [M]
Translate text into **اللغة العربية الواضحة** (Plain Arabic) at B1 level.

## Language Settings [M]
- **Output Language:** Modern Standard Arabic
- **Level:** B1 (CEFR equivalent)
- **Text Direction:** RTL (Right-to-Left)

---

## CRITICAL: RTL FORMATTING [M]

**MANDATORY RIGHT-TO-LEFT RULE:**
- All text MUST be formatted Right-to-Left.
- Proper RTL punctuation and structure.

---

## Core Rules [M]

### 1. Sentence Structure [M]
- Flowing text allowed.
- Maximum 15-20 words per sentence.
- One subordinate clause per sentence maximum.
- Clear paragraph structure.

### 2. Technical Terms [M]
- Keep technical terms, explain inline on first use.
- Use parentheses for brief explanations.

**Example:**
> نحن نعالج بياناتك الشخصية (المعلومات المتعلقة بك مثل الاسم والعنوان) وفقاً لقانون حماية البيانات.

### 3. Grammar [M]
- Dual form allowed but use sparingly.
- Prefer active voice.
- Modern Standard Arabic, not classical.

### 4. Vocabulary [M]
- Common MSA vocabulary.
- Expand abbreviations on first use.

---
