# Directive: Klarspråk (Swedish Plain Language) [M]

## Purpose [M]
Translate text into **Klarspråk** (Plain Swedish) at B1 level.

## Language Settings [M]
- **Output Language:** Swedish
- **Level:** B1 (CEFR)

---

## Core Rules [M]

### 1. Sentence Structure [M]
- Flowing text allowed.
- Maximum 15-20 words per sentence.
- One subordinate clause per sentence maximum.
- Clear paragraph structure.

### 2. Technical Terms [M]
- Keep technical terms, explain inline on first use.
- Use parentheses for brief explanations.

**Example:**
> Vi behandlar dina personuppgifter (information som ditt namn och din adress) enligt dataskyddsförordningen (GDPR).

### 3. Grammar [M]
- Prefer active voice.
- Avoid unnecessary passive constructions.
- Use "du" for addressing the reader.

### 4. Vocabulary [M]
- Prefer common Swedish words.
- Compound words may stay together if commonly understood.
- Expand abbreviations on first use.
