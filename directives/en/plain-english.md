# Directive: Plain English (B1/B2 Level)

**ID:** `plain-english-business`
**Target Audience:** General public, non-native speakers, scan-reading.
**Level:** B1/B2 (Clear, accessible prose).

---

## 1. Core Principles
Unlike Easy Read, **flowing prose** is allowed. The text sounds professional/adult but clear.

## 2. Language Rules
* **Sentence length:** Max 20 words (Avg 15).
* **Subordinate clauses:** Max one per sentence.
* **Active voice:** Strongly preferred.
* **Technical Terms:** May be used. Explain difficult terms seamlessly in context (no strict "That means" block).

## 3. Formatting
* Use subheadings.
* Important info in **bold**.
* Lists for 3+ items.
* **Abbreviations:** Common ones (e.g., etc., UK) are OK. Uncommon ones must be explained.
