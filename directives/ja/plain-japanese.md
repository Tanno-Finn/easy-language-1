# Directive: 明確な日本語 (Plain Japanese) [M]

## Purpose [M]
Defines rules for clear, comprehensible Japanese texts.
Less strict than やさしい日本語, but focused on clarity and precision.

**Priority:** Clarity and precision for a broad audience. [M]

## When to Use [M]
- Texts for the general public [M]
- Business communication [M]
- Technical documents for non-experts [M]

---

## Key Requirements [M]

### The Hybrid Rule [M]
Technical terms are **preserved** and explained when needed.

#### The "Tsumari" Anchor (つまり) [M]
Use the phrase **つまり：** for explanations.

**Format:**
```
**コンプライアンス**。

つまり：

企業が法令や規則を遵守すること。
```

---

## Writing System [M]

### Kanji Usage [M]
- Common Kanji (常用漢字) allowed [M]
- Avoid rare Kanji or explain them [C]
- For technical terms: preserve Kanji but explain [M]

### Katakana [M]
- Established foreign words in Katakana allowed [M]
- For less known terms: add brief explanation [C]

---

## Grammar & Sentence Structure [M]

### Sentence Structure [M]
- Prefer **clear structure** [M]
- **Moderate sentence length**: Maximum 40-50 characters recommended [M]
- Limit nested subordinate clauses [M]

### Verb Forms [M]
- **です/ます form** as standard [M]
- Appropriate politeness forms allowed [C]
- Passive only when necessary [C]

### Style [M]
- Prefer direct statements [M]
- Avoid unnecessary filler words [M]
- Concrete instead of abstract [M]

| Vague | Concrete |
|-------|----------|
| 適切に対応します | 3営業日以内に 回答します |
| なるべく早く | 今週中に |
| 検討します | 来週までに 決定します |

---

## Formatting [M]

### Paragraphs and Structure [M]
- Use logical paragraphs [M]
- Use headings for orientation [M]
- Use lists for multiple points [C]

### Numbers and Dates [M]
- Arabic numerals for numbers [M]
- Clear date formats [M]

| Less clear | Clear |
|------------|-------|
| 来月初旬 | 2月 3日まで |
| 数日以内 | 3日以内 |

---

## What to Avoid [M]

### Unnecessary Complexity [M]
- Overly formal expressions [M]
- Double negatives [M]
- Ambiguous formulations [M]

| Complex | Simple |
|---------|--------|
| ～ないわけではない | ～です |
| ご査収くださいますようお願い申し上げます | ご確認ください |
| 万障お繰り合わせの上 | ぜひ ご参加ください |

### Abbreviations [M]
Explain abbreviations on first occurrence:

> **GDPR**（EU一般データ保護規則）に基づき...

---

## Anti-Patterns [M]

### What NOT to do:
- Delete technical terms without explanation [M]
- Omit important details [M]
- Use unnecessarily complex politeness phrases [M]
- Use ambiguous time references [M]
- Use double negatives [M]

---

## Success Criteria [M]

### Quality Checklist:
- [ ] Technical terms preserved and explained [M]
- [ ] **"つまり：" correctly formatted** [M]
- [ ] Clear, moderate sentence length [M]
- [ ] Concrete statements [M]
- [ ] No ambiguous time references [M]
- [ ] Logical structure [M]
- [ ] Abbreviations explained [M]
