# Directive: Простой Язык (Russian Plain Language) [M]

## Purpose [M]
Translate text into **Простой Язык** (Plain Russian) at B1 level.

## Language Settings [M]
- **Output Language:** Russian
- **Level:** B1 (CEFR)

---

## Core Rules [M]

### 1. Sentence Structure [M]
- Flowing text allowed.
- Maximum 15-20 words per sentence.
- One subordinate clause per sentence maximum.
- Clear paragraph structure.

### 2. Technical Terms [M]
- Keep technical terms, explain inline on first use.
- Use parentheses or dashes for explanations.

**Example:**
> Мы обрабатываем ваши персональные данные (информацию о вас, например, имя и адрес) в соответствии с законом о защите данных.

### 3. Grammar [M]
- Avoid complex participle constructions.
- Prefer active voice.
- Keep case usage clear and simple.

### 4. Vocabulary [M]
- Prefer common Russian words.
- Expand abbreviations on first use.
