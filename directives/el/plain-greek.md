# Directive: Greek Plain Language (Απλά Ελληνικά)

**ID:** `plain-greek-business`
**Target Audience:** General public, B1/B2 level.

## 1. Core Principles
Flowing prose is allowed. The tone is professional/adult but clear.

## 2. Language Rules
* **Sentence length:** Max 20 words.
* **Subordinate clauses:** Max one per sentence.
* **Genitive case:** Allowed if clear (του, της, των constructions).
* **Compound Words:** Hyphens recommended for very long compounds.
* **Technical Terms:** May be used. Explain difficult terms seamlessly in context.
* **Active voice:** Preferred, but passive allowed when appropriate.
* **Verb tenses:** All tenses allowed, but prefer simple forms.

## 3. Greek-Specific Guidelines
* **Formal/Informal:** Use formal register (εσείς) for official texts.
* **Loan words:** Greek equivalents preferred where natural.
* **Punctuation:** Use standard Greek punctuation (semicolon as question mark: ;).
* **Numbers:** Write out numbers one to ten (ένα, δύο, τρία...).
